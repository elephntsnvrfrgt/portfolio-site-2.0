Country Music Hall of Fame

Preserve

Web | Concept, Design, & Motion

Located in Nashville, TN, Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum preserves the history and cultural importance of this music genre. They invited The Tombras Group, where I served as a visual designer, to pitch a tri-part site suite. In addition to the museum, CMHF manages two other historic entities: the legendary Hatch Show Print and Historic Studio B.

What started as a pitch, this responsive design concept became more of an exercise in process and thinking. I was able to extend the initial ideas into more thoughtful design systems.
